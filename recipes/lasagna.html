fricken LASAGNA, baby!

Lasagna is a miracle of a food. It has layers.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk

HOW2MAKE

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Make bechamel -- no riccota here!
  3. Boil noodles
  4. Layer noodles, bechamel, sauce, and cheese
  5. Bake for like 40 minutes
